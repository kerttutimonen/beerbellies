Our story

Brewing co is dedicated to making great beer and has won multiple awards for doing so!

The brewery was founded by the Brewing brothers: Uffi and Jesper Bering, in November 2017.

The company is headquartered in Nørrebro, Copenhagen.

At our core of origin, we are a brewing company. But we’re much more than that. Brewing co is a socio-innovative business, working on developing and spreading new social business understandings.
